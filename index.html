What is MatchUp about?

MatchUp is a concept for an application that was conceptualized by a group of first-year students during an entrepreneurship course at ESSEC Business School. The aim of this app is to connect ESSEC students with similar interests and values, promoting collective activities and fostering a sense of community among students aged 17 to 24. By addressing the potential for exclusion arising from the diverse backgrounds of ESSEC students, MatchUp seeks to enhance the overall student experience and create a more inclusive environment.
